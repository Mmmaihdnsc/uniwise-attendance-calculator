Saveetha Engineering College Attendance Regulations

Saveetha Engineering College (SEC), an autonomous institution affiliated with Anna University, operates a choice-based credit system. In line with the Anna University attendance rule framework, students must secure a minimum of 75% attendance overall to write the final semester exams.

Using our attendance calculator college widget, you can find out if you are safe or in the warning zone. If you have been participating in lab works and lectures but are wondering "how many classes can I miss?", UniWise computes your allowances instantly using the 75% attendance calculator formula.

Saveetha Condonation Limits

Condonation is offered for students falling within the 65% to 74% bracket under valid circumstances (hospitalization, emergency, or college representing OD) after paying the condonation fine. Under 65% results in course debarment, and you must repeat the classes in next year's cycle. Check the main homepage to reset values.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the debarment rule at Saveetha Engineering College?
Students with attendance below 65% are debarred. They cannot write the semester end examinations and must re-register for the course when it is next offered.
Does Saveetha accept medical certificates for shortages?
Yes, medical certificates are accepted to apply for condonation if your final attendance is between 65% and 74%.
