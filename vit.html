VIT University Attendance Regulations & FFCS Rules

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) has a strict attendance policy under the Fully Flexible Credit System (FFCS). Regular students must maintain a minimum of 80% attendance in each course. A medical condonation may lower this limit to 75% in case of severe illness, but dropping below 75% results in automatic debarment. However, students with a CGPA of 9.0 or higher enjoy an attendance waiver and are exempt from the 80% rule.

How is Attendance Calculated in Engineering Colleges?

VIT calculates attendance as the percentage of actual hours attended vs total conducted sessions. Our VIT attendance calculator helps you run this math instantly. For other institutions, check the main homepage to run custom parameters.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many classes can I miss in VIT?
Under the 80% rule, you can miss up to 20% of the total sessions. If 40 sessions are conducted, you can safely miss 8.
What happens if attendance is below 75% at VIT?
If your attendance drops below 75%, there is no condonation, and the student is debarred from the course.
How is attendance calculated in engineering colleges?
Your college attendance is calculated as: (Total attended hours / Total conducted hours) * 100. Our tool computes this in real-time.
