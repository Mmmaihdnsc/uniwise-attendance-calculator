VIT University Attendance Rules & Exemption Details

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) implements a Fully Flexible Credit System (FFCS) monitored through the VTOP student portal. VIT differs from standard institutions by enforcing an **80% default attendance rule** for theory and practical courses.

For students checking "how many classes can I miss" to stay above debarment, maintaining an 80% average is critical. If attendance falls below 80% down to 75%, a medical certificate might get you condoned. Under 75%, debarment is automatic and absolute.

The 9.0 CGPA Exemption (9 Pointers)

Exemptions are granted under VIT rules for students with an overall CGPA of **9.0 or above**. These high-performing students have their attendance requirements waived and can miss classes safely. However, standard students must continue using the 80% attendance calculator features to monitor margins.

For standard calculations, check out the UniWise home portal.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if a student is debarred in VIT?
Debarred students are not permitted to write the Term End Exams (TEE) for that subject. They receive a debarred status and must register to clear the subject as a repeat course in future semesters.
Can I use this for VIT Chennai or Bhopal campuses?
Yes, all VIT campuses (Vellore, Chennai, AP, Bhopal) follow the same VTOP-managed 80% default attendance guidelines.
