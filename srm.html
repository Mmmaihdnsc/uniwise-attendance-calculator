SRM IST Attendance Policies & Regulations

At SRM Institute of Science and Technology, maintaining attendance is vital for writing end semester exams. SRM students use our attendance calculator college tracker to stay on top of the mandatory 75% attendance calculator rules. By typing your values in, you'll know precisely where you stand.

If you're asking yourself "how many classes can I miss?", remember that SRM checks attendance on a subject-by-subject basis via the Academia student portal. Keeping a healthy percentage of 75% or higher guarantees a stress-free exam entry.

SRM Condonation Zone (65% to 74%)

If your percentage falls into this bracket, you must submit a medical certificate or sports OD certificate alongside a condonation fee. Debarment happens if attendance goes below 65%, prompting students to re-register for the course or complete it during summer classes.

For more detailed calculations, you can visit the main calculator homepage or check our other guides.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the debarment rule at SRM IST?
Students with attendance below 65% in any subject are debarred from the exam in that course. They will receive an 'I' grade and must re-register to repeat the subject.
Can I edit my required attendance on the SRM calculator?
Yes, although the default preset is 75%, you can change the target percentage manually to track condonation limits (65%) or higher targets (e.g. 90%).
