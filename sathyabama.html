Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology Attendance Rules

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology enforces a strict class presence framework. Under university regulations, students need to maintain a 75% attendance calculator score in each subject to ensure hall ticket release.

Using our attendance calculator college widget, Sathyabama students can see their status in real-time. If you find your values dropping, the tool calculates exactly how many consecutive classes you must attend to catch up. A link to the main homepage helps you try other presets.

Sathyabama Condonation & Shortage Details

Shortages below 75% down to 65% are subject to a condonation fine upon submitting official medical letters. Below 65%, no condonation will be approved, meaning the student is debarred from exams and must register to repeat the semester courses.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the debarment rule at Sathyabama?
Students with overall attendance below 65% are debarred. They are not allowed to sit for final semester examinations and must register for the courses in later semesters.
How can I check my attendance at Sathyabama?
Students can check their subject-wise attendance logs directly on the official Sathyabama student portal.
