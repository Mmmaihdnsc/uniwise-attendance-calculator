Frequently Asked Questions

What is a college attendance calculator?
A college attendance calculator is a tool designed to help students track class attendance. By inputting total classes conducted and classes attended, it displays your percentage, bunk limit, and recovery plan.
How many classes can I miss in college?
Under the standard 75% attendance rule, you can safely miss up to 25% of the total sessions conducted. For instance, if 80 sessions are conducted, you can safely miss 20 classes.
What happens if attendance is below 75% in India?
If your attendance is between 65% and 74%, you fall under the condonation zone. You must present official medical certificates and pay a fine to receive your exam hall ticket. Below 65%, you are debarred from examinations.
Can I recover low attendance?
Yes, you can recover by attending consecutive upcoming classes without missing any. Our Recovery Planner tab calculates the exact number of consecutive sessions needed.
How is attendance calculated in engineering colleges in India?
It is calculated using the formula: (Hours Attended / Total Hours Conducted) * 100. Our tool uses this system to provide an instant breakdown of your academic buffers.
